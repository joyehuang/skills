# Script Generation

**核心原则**：旁白是视频的灵魂，不是"注释"。必须完整、可直接照读、信息充实。

## ⚠️ CRITICAL: Language Requirement

**ALL NARRATIONS MUST BE WRITTEN IN ENGLISH**

- ✅ Write all narrations in English, regardless of input article language
- ✅ This applies to ALL scenes (hook, textDisplay, numberComparison, threeColumns, caseStudy, cta)
- ✅ Use natural, conversational English (American English preferred)
- ❌ Do NOT write narrations in Chinese, Japanese, or any other language
- ❌ Do NOT mix languages within narrations

**Why English?**
- International audience reach
- TTS compatibility across platforms
- Easier translation to other languages if needed
- Professional standard for global content

## Critical: 旁白质量标准

### ❌ 常见错误（必须避免）

```
❌ 错误示范1：过于简短
场景时长：20秒
旁白："为什么不找真人做研究？三个原因。"（10字）
问题：20秒场景只说10字，剩余时间空白

❌ 错误示范2：不完整
场景时长：15秒
旁白："Solar panels pay for themselves."（5 words）
问题：15秒场景只说5个词，内容严重不足

❌ 错误示范3：缺少细节
场景时长：18秒
旁白："安装很快，维护很少。"（7字）
问题：没有具体数据，无法说服观众
```

### ✅ 正确示范

```
✅ 正确做法：完整、具体、可直接照读

场景时长：20秒
旁白："你是不是也以为：做研究只能找真人？结论先说：不是AI比真人更好，
      而是——真人调研在现实里经常慢、贵，甚至做不到。"（58字）

场景时长：18秒
旁白："Installation is quick too. Professional solar installation typically takes
      just one to three days. One long weekend, and your home becomes an
      energy-producing powerhouse."（25 words = ~10s, will expand to 50 words = 20s）

✅ 关键特点：
- 完整的句子，有开头、过程、结论
- 包含具体数据和例子
- 长度匹配场景时长
- 可以拿起来就照着读，无需解读
```

## Target Length Per Scene (Baseline, Before Expansion)

| Scene Type | Duration | Baseline Words | Expanded Words (Stage 4) | 中文字数 |
|-----------|----------|---------------|------------------------|---------|
| Hook | 5-8s | 10-20 words | 18-36 words | 12-20字 |
| TextDisplay | 15-20s | 25-40 words | 50-80 words | 50-70字 |
| NumberComparison | 12-18s | 20-30 words | 40-60 words | 40-60字 |
| ThreeColumns | 15-20s | 25-40 words | 50-80 words | 50-70字 |
| CaseStudy | 20-30s | 35-50 words | 70-100 words | 70-100字 |
| CTA | 8-12s | 15-25 words | 30-50 words | 30-40字 |

**Formula**: `target_words = scene_duration (seconds) × 2.5`

**Critical**: 旁白长度必须填满场景时长。如果不够，就加细节、例子、数据。

## Writing Guidelines

### 1. 完整性原则（最重要）

每个场景的旁白必须：
- ✅ 自成完整语义单元（有开头、过程、结论）
- ✅ 包含具体信息（数据、例子、对比）
- ✅ 长度匹配场景时长（不能太短）
- ✅ 可以独立理解（不依赖其他场景）

### 2. 口语化表达

**技巧**：从书面语改写成口语

```
原文（书面语）：
"真人调研的隐藏成本包括招募费用、时间成本、平台服务费等，
总成本可达750-3100美元。"

旁白稿（口语化）：
"隐藏成本加起来，几百到几千美元很常见。
便宜样本往往还不是你的目标人群。"
```

**要点**：
- 用短句（每句≤20字）
- 用常用词（避免专业术语）
- 用主动语态（"你会"而非"可以被"）
- 用问答结构（"为什么？因为..."）

### 3. 信息密度

**每10秒传递1个核心信息点**

```
✅ 好的信息密度（18秒场景）：
"Installation is quick too. [信息点1：快]
Professional solar installation typically takes just 1 to 3 days. [信息点2：具体时长]
One long weekend, and your home becomes an energy-producing powerhouse. [信息点3：转变]"

❌ 差的信息密度（18秒场景）：
"Solar panels are installed on your roof. It's a professional service."
（18秒只传递了1个模糊信息）
```

**加信息的方法**：
- 加数据："快" → "1-3天"
- 加对比："便宜" → "比真人便宜70%"
- 加例子："效果好" → "Johnson家省了$11,000"
- 加具体化："安装" → "专业团队到你家，测量、安装、验收"

### 4. 场景特定写法

#### Hook (5-8秒)：反常识开场

```
结构：[反常识陈述] + [引发好奇]

✅ 示例：
"What if I told you your roof could actually make you money while you sleep?
Sound too good to be true?"

技巧：
- 用"What if..."开头
- 用"你是不是也以为..."开头
- 结尾留悬念
- 不要解释，只抛问题
```

#### TextDisplay (15-20秒)：核心信息展示

```
结构：[核心观点] + [支撑论据] + [强化结论]

✅ 示例：
"Solar energy isn't just better for the planet—it's way better for your wallet too.
You can cut your monthly energy bills by up to 70%, while also increasing your home's
resale value. It's a win-win."

技巧：
- 第一句直接说结论
- 第二句给具体数据
- 最后强化（"It's a win-win"）
```

#### NumberComparison (12-18秒)：数据对比

```
结构：[数据对比] + [意义解释]

✅ 示例：
"Here's the best part: solar panels actually pay for themselves in just 6 to 8 years.
With average savings of $1,500 every year and a 30% federal tax credit, you'll break
even faster than you think."

技巧：
- 先说数字，再解释意义
- 用"从X到Y"的对比
- 加"也就是说..."的通俗化
```

#### ThreeColumns (15-20秒)：三个要点

```
结构：[要点1] + [要点2] + [要点3] + [总结]

✅ 示例：
"And get this: today's solar panels come with 25-year performance warranties.
They require minimal maintenance—just occasional cleaning. And they deliver
lasting value for decades to come."

技巧：
- 用"First...Second...Third..."
- 用"And..."连接
- 最后总结升华
```

#### CaseStudy (20-30秒)：故事叙述

```
结构：[背景] + [过程] + [结果] + [数据]

✅ 示例：
"Take the Johnson family in Phoenix. They installed solar in 2020.
Their monthly bill dropped from $250 to just $60. Over 5 years, they've saved
over $11,000. And when they sold their home last year, they got $20,000 more
than comparable homes on their street."

技巧：
- 用真实姓名（或虚构但具体）
- 给具体时间、地点
- 给前后对比数据
- 给最终结果
```

#### CTA (8-12秒)：行动号召

```
结构：[行动指令] + [联系方式] + [紧迫感]

✅ 示例：
"Ready to start saving money? Schedule your free solar assessment today at
GreenEnergy.com/free. Limited slots available this month—don't wait!"

技巧：
- 用动词开头（"Schedule", "Visit", "Download"）
- 重复联系方式（GreenEnergy.com/free）
- 加紧迫感（"Limited", "Today", "Don't wait"）
```

## TTS优化

### 1. 拼写清晰

```
✅ 拼出难读词：
- "St. Louis" → "Saint Louis"
- "Dr. Smith" → "Doctor Smith"
- "etc." → "and so on"

✅ 拼出10以下数字：
- "3 days" → "three days"
- "5 benefits" → "five benefits"

✅ 避免缩写：
- "CEO" → "C E O"（逐字母读）或 "chief executive officer"
- "AI" → "A I"
```

### 2. 标点规范

```
✅ 明确停顿：
- 用句号表示完整停顿
- 用逗号表示短暂停顿
- 用破折号表示转折（"——"）

❌ 避免混淆：
- 不要用多个问号（"???"）
- 不要用省略号（"..."）TTS可能读出"dot dot dot"
```

### 3. 重复关键信息

```
✅ 重要信息说两遍：
"Visit GreenEnergy.com/free. That's GreenEnergy.com/free."

✅ 数字说两种方式：
"Six to eight years. That's 6-8 years total."
```

## 转场设计

场景之间必须有自然过渡：

```
❌ 生硬转场：
Scene 2 ends: "Solar cuts your bills by 70%."
Scene 3 starts: "Panels last 25 years."

✅ 流畅转场：
Scene 2 ends: "Solar cuts your bills by 70%. But how long until you break even?"
Scene 3 starts: "Great question. Solar panels actually pay for themselves in
just 6 to 8 years. And they last 25 years..."
```

**转场技巧**：
- 用问题引入下一场景
- 用"And here's the best part..."
- 用"Plus..."、"What's more..."
- 用"Remember that 70% savings? Here's how..."

## Quality Checklist

写完每个场景的旁白后，检查：

### 完整性
- [ ] 长度匹配场景时长（±2秒）
- [ ] 包含具体数据或例子
- [ ] 自成完整语义单元
- [ ] 可以独立理解

### 可执行性
- [ ] 可以直接照读，无需解读
- [ ] 没有需要"脑补"的部分
- [ ] TTS友好（无生僻词、缩写）
- [ ] 标点清晰

### 信息密度
- [ ] 每10秒≥1个信息点
- [ ] 没有废话或重复
- [ ] 数据具体（不是"很多"而是"70%"）

### 口语化
- [ ] 像说话一样自然
- [ ] 句子≤20字（中文）或15 words（英文）
- [ ] 用主动语态
- [ ] 用常用词

## 常见错误修正

### 错误1：旁白过短

```
❌ 问题：
Scene duration: 18s
Narration: "Solar is good for the environment."（7 words = 2.8s）
Gap: 15.2s of silence!

✅ 修正：
Scene duration: 18s
Narration: "Of course, we can't ignore the environmental benefits. By switching
to solar, the average household reduces its carbon footprint by 3-4 tons of CO2
per year. That's equivalent to planting 100 trees annually. You're not just
saving money—you're helping save the planet."（48 words = ~19s after expansion）
```

### 错误2：缺少具体性

```
❌ 问题：
"Installation is fast and easy."

✅ 修正：
"Professional solar installation typically takes just 1 to 3 days. That's it.
One long weekend, and your home is transformed into an energy-producing powerhouse."
```

### 错误3：书面语过重

```
❌ 问题：
"Photovoltaic systems demonstrate exceptional longevity and require minimal maintenance interventions."

✅ 修正：
"Today's solar panels are built to last 25 to 30 years. They require minimal
maintenance—just occasional cleaning. That's decades of reliable, clean energy."
```
